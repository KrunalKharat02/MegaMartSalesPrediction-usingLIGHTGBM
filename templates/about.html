{% endif %} About Mega Mart Sales Prediction Project

Welcome to the about page of our Mega Mart Sales Prediction Project!

Our project is aimed at helping store managers to make data-driven decisions by predicting future sales based on historical sales data. The Mega Mart dataset contains sales data of 1559 products across 10 outlets from the year 1985 to 2009.

We used various machine learning algorithms, including linear regression, decision trees, random forests, XGboost, LightGBM and so on to train our model and select the best-performing algorithm.

We also incorporated feature engineering techniques to extract meaningful insights from the dataset, including identifying key product and outlet attributes that influence sales (Please Refer to the analysis page). Our final model achieved an impressive RSME OF 1068.796699, which makes it a reliable tool for sales prediction.

We are confident that our Mega Mart Sales Prediction Project will be a valuable asset to store managers, helping them optimize their inventory management, pricing strategies, and overall sales performance.
